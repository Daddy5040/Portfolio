# Project Name

Maherison 3D Portfolio

Working Title:
Project Atlas

---

# Mission

Create a premium digital portfolio that showcases high-end CGI product visualization services for modern brands and Amazon sellers.

The portfolio is not intended to demonstrate software proficiency alone. Its primary objective is to communicate value, increase trust, and convert visitors into paying clients.

Every page, animation, interaction, and visual decision must reinforce the perception of quality, professionalism, and commercial impact.

---

# Vision

To become a recognized specialist in premium product visualization, helping brands transform ordinary products into compelling visual experiences that increase perceived value and improve sales performance.

The website itself should be a demonstration of the quality clients can expect.

---

# Primary Goal

Generate qualified leads and convert visitors into clients seeking professional CGI product visualization services.

The portfolio is a commercial tool before being an artistic gallery.

# Confidential Work

Professional experience involving confidential commercial projects must not expose client-sensitive information.

When appropriate, describe confidential work in general terms while emphasizing the acquired expertise.

Example:

"Worked on confidential visualization projects for international clients."

---

# Success Criteria

The portfolio is considered successful if visitors immediately understand:

• What services are offered.

• Why CGI provides value.

• Why Maherison is different.

• How to start a project.

Within the first 30 seconds of navigation, the visitor should trust the quality of the services offered.
