# Brand Guidelines
Version 1.0

---

# Brand Identity

Brand Name

Maherison Daddy

The brand represents a premium CGI Product Visualization Specialist focused on helping brands and Amazon sellers present their products through photorealistic visuals.

The identity should communicate trust, precision, luxury and commercial value.

---

# Brand Personality

Professional

Premium

Minimal

Elegant

Commercial

Reliable

Creative

Confident

Attention to Detail

Every interaction should reinforce the feeling of working with a premium visualization studio.

---

# Brand Position

Maherison Daddy is not presented as a freelance 3D artist.

He is positioned as a Product Visualization Specialist helping businesses increase the perceived value of their products through high-end CGI.

The communication must always focus on client value rather than software skills.

---

Large white space.

Large imagery.

Minimal distractions.

# Imagery

Large hero renders.

Minimal cropping.

High resolution.

Consistent lighting.

Consistent color grading.

The renders must dominate the composition.

Text exists to support the visuals, not the opposite.

---

# Tone of Voice

Luxury Creative Studio

Professional

Business-focused

Minimal

Clear

Confident

Avoid unnecessary technical jargon.

Speak about business value whenever possible.

---

# Service Priority

1. Amazon A+ Content

2. Packaging Visualization

3. Product Rendering

4. Lifestyle Images

5. Hero Images

6. Product Animation

---

# Software Display

Software should only appear inside:

About Page

Case Studies

Project Details

Avoid highlighting software on the Home page.

Clients buy results, not software.

---

# Photography Style

Dark backgrounds.

Soft studio lighting.

Luxury materials.

Clean compositions.

Minimal clutter.

Premium atmosphere.

---

# UI Principles

Every page should answer three questions within five seconds:

What do you do?

Why should I trust you?

How can I contact you?

---

# What We Never Do

Never overload pages.

Never use excessive animations.

Never use bright colorful gradients.

Never let the interface overshadow the renders.

Never present the portfolio as a software showcase.

---

# Guiding Principle

The interface exists to elevate the product, never to compete with it.

Every design decision must increase trust, perceived value and professionalism.
